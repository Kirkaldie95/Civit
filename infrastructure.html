Tender & Construction

Pre-Construction Surveys and Site Readiness:

We help clients de-risk development through the coordination and review of key site surveys and assessments ahead of construction or procurement.

  • Specification and procurement of topographical and utility surveys
  • CCTV drainage surveys and condition reporting
  • Site walkovers and engineering appraisals
  • Survey interpretation and integration into design models
  • Early contractor involvement and buildability reviews

Tender Support and Technical Packs:

Our team supports clients, contractors, and design teams in the preparation of clear, coordinated, and cost-effective tender documentation.

  • Preparation of civil engineering tender and construction drawing packages
  • Technical specifications and scope of works for highways, drainage, and earthworks
  • Bill of quantities (BoQ) inputs and tender queries support
  • Value engineering advice and phasing considerations
  • Responses to contractor queries and technical clarifications

On-Site Support and RFI Responses:

We remain actively involved during construction, providing prompt and practical support to ensure the design intent is maintained and issues are quickly resolved.

  • Response to Requests for Information (RFIs) and design clarifications
  • Attendance at site and progress meetings
  • Review of contractor proposals and alternative solutions
  • Issuing of updated construction details and sketches as required
  • Support during inspections, sign-offs, and adoption processes
