Drainage Services

At Southern Civil Engineering Consultancy, we provide comprehensive drainage consultancy services that support developments from feasibility through to completion. Our solutions are aligned with planning policies, industry standards, and sustainable development goals.

  • Flood Risk Assessments (FRA): Detailed assessments to support planning applications, identifying flood risks and recommending mitigation strategies in line with Environment Agency requirements.
  • Sustainable Drainage Systems (SuDS) Strategies: Concept and detailed design of SuDS, integrating permeable paving, swales, attenuation basins, and green roofs to manage surface water sustainably.
  • Drainage Design (Surface & Foul): Full network design for new and existing developments, including pipe sizing, manhole schedules, and long sections using industry-standard software (e.g., MicroDrainage, InfoDrainage).
  • Pre-planning and Feasibility Reports: Early-stage advice to inform development layout and viability.
  • Adoption Drawings & Sewer Agreements (Section 104, 106 & 185): Preparation of technical drawings and liaison with water authorities to ensure seamless adoption and connections.
  • Construction Support & As-Built Drawings: On-site support, drainage reviews during construction, and production of final as-built documentation.

Highway Services

Our highways team delivers safe, compliant, and efficient access solutions that support the smooth approval of planning applications and successful site delivery.

  • Access Design & Junction Layouts: Detailed access design for vehicles and pedestrians, including visibility splays and swept path analysis.
  • Transport Statements & Assessments: Reports supporting planning applications, assessing traffic impacts and demonstrating accessibility.
  • Road Safety Audits: Independent audits to identify potential safety issues and improve highway design.
  • Section 278 & 38 Agreements: Design and coordination of highway works for adoption or improvements, including liaison with Local Highway Authorities.
  • Construction Traffic Management Plans: Planning safe and efficient site access and movement during the construction phase.

Infrastructure Services

Our infrastructure services ensure that developments are delivered with robust and cost-effective servicing strategies that meet both regulatory and practical requirements.

  • Utilities Coordination & Diversions: Review of existing services, coordination of new utility layouts, and management of diversion requirements.
  • Earthworks & Site Leveling Strategy: Cut and fill analysis to inform cost-effective and sustainable site preparation.
  • Estate Road & Plot Drainage Layouts: Integrated design of access roads, parking, and drainage for residential and commercial developments.
  • Construction Detailing & Specification: Comprehensive technical detailing for tender and construction stages.
