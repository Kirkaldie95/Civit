Professional Civil Engineering services to support developments through planning and construction.

Planning & Feasibility

We provide expert Flood Risk Assessment (FRA) and Sustainable Urban Drainage Strategy (SuDS) reports for planning. Our team have extensive experience working with local authorities across the UK and tailoring deliverables to align and derisk the planning process.
Civit provides early stage feasibility review services, indentifying key risks early doors and inputting on wider masterplans.

Highways and Infrastructure

The Civit team have lead highway design packages for commercial, industrial and residential developments. Designing spine roads, site access junctions and obtaining the required statutory approvals (S38, S278) where necessary.
We offer external level design services ensuring Part M / DDA compliance, balancing cut and fill or ensuring flood plane compensation is provided.

Learn more

Tender and Construction

The Civit team is highly experienced in leading civil engineering projects through the construction phase, providing proactive support from pre-start to completion. We work closely with contractors, design teams, and approving authorities to ensure that designs are delivered efficiently on site, resolving technical challenges and maintaining compliance with all relevant standards and regulations.
