Planning and Feasibility

Flood Risk Assessments (FRAs):

Understanding and mitigating flood risk is essential to gaining planning approval and ensuring long-term resilience. We deliver detailed assessments in line with national planning policy and Environment Agency requirements.

  • Site-specific Flood Risk Assessments (FRA)
  • Sequential and Exception Test support
  • Liaison with Lead Local Flood Authorities (LLFAs) and the Environment Agency
  • Flood mitigation strategies and design recommendations
  • Surface water and fluvial risk modelling

Sustainable Drainage Systems (SuDS) Strategies:

Effective drainage planning enhances sustainability, reduces flood risk, and is a planning requirement for most developments. Our SuDS strategies combine technical rigour with design integration.

  • Preliminary SuDS strategy and outline drainage design
  • Greenfield/brownfield runoff assessments
  • Drainage catchment mapping and hydraulic calculations
  • Integration of SuDS into landscape and masterplanning
  • Design for compliance with local and national SuDS standards (CIRIA, DEFRA, etc.)

Feasibility Studies:

Before detailed design begins, we assess technical and regulatory viability to de-risk your project from the outset.

  • Initial site appraisal and desk-based flood screening
  • Utility and infrastructure constraints mapping
  • Review of planning policy and development constraints
  • Early-stage consultation with relevant authorities
  • Recommendations to inform land acquisition or early investment decisions
